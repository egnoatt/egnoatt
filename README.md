### [WRIP-Radar](https://github.com/egnoatt/wrip-radar)

A manual-first strategic intelligence radar designed to observe long-term technological, scientific, industrial, regulatory, social, and geopolitical signals.

WRIP-Radar is not an automatic trend scanner. It is a governed review system that helps transform weak signals, emerging patterns, and fragmented information into structured strategic awareness.

The project keeps human judgment central, avoids unnecessary automation, and supports monthly reviews without turning governance into a blocking activity.

**Focus areas:**  
Strategic intelligence · weak signals · future trends · governance · human judgment · decision support

## My Approach

I build digital systems around four principles:

1. **Clarity before automation**  
   Technology should make decisions easier to understand, not more opaque.

2. **Governance by design**  
   AI and data systems need traceability, boundaries, and human oversight.

3. **Execution matters**  
   A good idea only creates value when it becomes a usable, maintainable system.

4. **Human judgment stays central**  
   AI should support people, not replace accountability.
